Info

As a technical enthusiast, the website was created to show my experience in front-end web development with the background of education and careers. A mentor of mine mentions "Nicole is very organized and is able to handle multiple tasks and function simultaneously to meet the deliverable dates and details and content expected".

Having a mentor say such encouraging words led me to believe I can make this happen. That I am a warrior in life and have a 'can do' attitude.

Overall, the website is to bring visibility to potential employers what I can accomplish in my education and career.

The Challenge

Many challenges while building the website. For starters, I am not using to writing about myself nor my history of education and careers on the world wide web. Overall, the biggest challenge is being comfortable enough to create this site to elicit myself digitally.

The Solution

To create a website that would allow recruiters to have digital visibility to what I can accomplish in development and in my career.

Responsibilities

To be the developer, project manager, and analyst of the site. Maintaining the site is a key responsibility to ensure all information is valid.
