Info

At the University of Missouri - St. Louis (UMSL), the students majoring in Information Systems is required to take a capstone class called Systems Design. In this class, the students are required to find a real client with a real challenge. The students are to take this challenge and propose the solution for the client to ideally use.

Within my group, we brainstormed ideas on whom could be our client. One member of my group, recommended the Green Park Lutheran School's Before Care and After Care Program. The Green Park Lutheran School was experiencing process pains with the Before Care and After Care program for tracking when students were arriving and leaving.

Please see Project Manager under Responsibilities for what I did.

The Challenge

Our client's challenge was using an old fashion system of using pen and paper for everything. By utilizing the pen and paper system it caused errors and difficulties. Errors that happened were from user-error by not recording times correctly. Due to this error, the most difficult part was going back and manually changing on the paper before submitting the records for requesting payments. Storage was down by a bankers box and only storing the material for a certain amount of time. Due to limited space it was difficult for them to even store a lot of their paperwork.

The Solution

My team of six proposed a solution with today's technology. We built out an entire system that will help eliminate paper usage and 90% non-human error. The system allows for the administrators to add, remove, and manage their database. Database includes the families enrolled for the program and the items for each day (i.e. popsicles).

Responsibilities

Project Manager: To understand the problem and work with the team in order to propose a solution. Assign roles based on the skills of each team member into either an analyst role, database admin, and developer. Worked with the analyst in order to complete documentation. Manage the team to ensure deliverables are meet and that we are on track with the project.

Analysts: To understand the problem and work with the project manager to complete documentation for the project. Documentation consisting of Request for Proposal, Information System Plan, Internal Documentation, and External Documentation.

Database Specialist: To understand the problem and work with the developer in creating a database for the website.

Developer: To understand the problem that the website is supposed to solve, design a solution, develop the site and test before releasing to customers.
